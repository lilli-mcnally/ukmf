The West Midlands has long been recognised as a powerhouse of UK manufacturing. With a rich industrial heritage and a modern reputation for innovation, the region is home to world-leading businesses in automotive, aerospace, advanced engineering, and cutting-edge technologies.

From major manufacturers to agile SMEs, the West Midlands continues to drive economic growth, productivity, and skills development—and this is the very reason why we are in the perfect location to inspire the next generation of makers, engineers, and problem-solvers and ensure the future is bright for West Midlands Manufacturers.

So why not get involved—as an exhibitor or sponsor—and be part of a powerful movement to close the skills gap and build a more inclusive, accessible future for our manufacturing industry in the West Midlands
